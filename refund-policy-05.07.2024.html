Refund Policy

If for any reason you are not happy with a product or a service you bought from extensions.ee, you can request a refund within 30 days after the payment.

After issuing a refund, your access to the product or the service will be blocked and you will not be allowed to purchase it again in future.

To request a refund, visit https://paddle.net/ and follow the instructions there.

Changes To Our Refund Policy

Should we make any changes to this document, those changes will be posted here: https://extensions.ee/refund-policy.html

In case of changes, you will be bound by the old Refund Policy that was in force on the date of your payment.
